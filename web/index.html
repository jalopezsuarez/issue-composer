How it works

1

Open the app

Load the page on your phone or add it to the home screen as a web app.

2

Add your keys

Paste a GitHub token and your OpenAI-compatible LLM settings in Settings.

3

Compose with AI

Write a note, choose a mode, and let the AI draft the title and body.

4

Publish & manage

Publish the issue, move it on the board and reply to comments.

No backend. No telemetry.

Your tokens stay on your device

Issue Composer is a single static file. Every request goes directly from your browser to the GitHub API and to your LLM provider. Your credentials live only in your browser's local storage.
